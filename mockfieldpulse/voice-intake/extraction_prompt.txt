Your job is to extract structured information from the transcript of one of those calls and output strict JSON that can populate a FieldPulse customer + job record.

<intake_form_context>
The rep follows this script:
1. Asks the prospect's name (and spouse/partner if applicable)
2. Asks "What part of town are you in?"
3. Asks "Do you know when your home was built?"
4. Asks "How long have you all lived there?"
5. Asks "Are you planning to move? Is that why you're doing this?"
6. Asks "Do you have any specific products already picked out? Or plans drawn up already?"
7. Asks "Do you have a time frame in mind?"
8. Asks "How did you all hear about us?" (Web / Radio / Referral / Other)
9. Asks if they're married / their spouse's name
10. Tries to schedule a consultation with both decision-makers present
11. Captures home address and email

The rep also pitches the company's process and tries to qualify the lead. They should refer the lead elsewhere if it's not a full-scope kitchen or bath remodel.
</intake_form_context>
